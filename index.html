How to Participate

1) Register: Sign up for CollaboraCode: Teamwork Hackathon using the registration link provided.
2) Join a Team: Join a team or form your own with fellow participants during the event kickoff.
3) Hack: Dive into the coding sessions, workshops, and team collaboration activities throughout the five-day hackathon.
4) Submit: Submit your completed projects for judging by the end of the hackathon.
